FAQ

Questions we get asked

Answers to the most common things people want to know before starting a project with us.

How long does a typical project take? ▼
It depends on complexity. A landing page can be live in 5–7 days. A full SaaS platform with AI integrations typically takes 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline after our discovery call.
Do you work with international clients? ▼
Absolutely. We have clients across India, the US, UK, UAE, and Southeast Asia. All our communication and collaboration is fully remote-friendly.
What technologies do you specialize in? ▼
We work primarily with Angular, React, Next.js, and Vue for frontend. Node.js, Python, and Go for backend. For AI, we integrate OpenAI, Anthropic, and custom fine-tuned models depending on the use case.
Can you take over an existing project? ▼
Yes — we regularly onboard onto existing codebases. We'll do a technical audit first to understand the architecture, then propose the most efficient path forward.
What does hosting with NexaForge include? ▼
All hosting plans include SSL certificates, global CDN, DDoS protection, automated backups, uptime monitoring, and zero-downtime deployments across 48 edge locations worldwide.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch? ▼
Yes. We offer monthly retainer plans for ongoing development, maintenance, and performance optimization. Many clients transition to a long-term partnership after the initial project.
How do you handle NDAs and IP ownership? ▼
You own 100% of the code and assets we build for you. We're happy to sign NDAs before any discussions. Our contracts are straightforward, client-friendly, and reviewed by legal counsel.
What's your payment structure? ▼
Typically 40% upfront, 30% at midpoint milestone, and 30% on final delivery. For larger enterprise projects, we can arrange monthly billing or custom milestone-based payment schedules.
